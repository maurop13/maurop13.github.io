Who am I

Mauricio Perez M, Emeryville, CA, USA

I'm a curious and passionate problem solver. I enjoy analyzing situations and problems, going into their root causes, and producing insights on their effects over other situations. I am very data and fact oriented, trying to gather as much information as possible, as well as analyzing it to understand commonalities and outlier data that might explain specific processes or behaviours of a set of interacting actors in a problem.

I'm a Psychologist and Industrial Engineer by academical formation, and a practical solutioner molded by my professional ventures. I'm most interested in aiding companies achieve their business strategy through a mix of data gathering and analytics, expert and stakeholder interviews and interaction, and research. I've refined the tools I have learnt through both of my degrees to incorporate hard data, people motivations and qualitative information, and generate an integral understanding of phenomenons.

I also hold a graduate degree in Human Talent Management and did a 6 months Data Analytics Bootcamp. I pursued both of these to get a better understanding of the underlying dynamics of people interacting with businesses and to hone my data analytics skills in a data information filled world.

In this webpage you will find different projects I've worked on while going through a Data Analytics Bootcamp, links to my relevant social media and a CV/resume in case you're interested in knowing more about me and/or contacting me.
